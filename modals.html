News #2


New

It has been 2 months since we made the Datalab digital kick-off. Today is a very important day for us. We celebrate the version 0.2 of sparrow, our artificial intelligence platform, built thanks to your feedback. Today, we also want to give you something. We are releasing all our current projects to you, as an InnerSource initiative. We do believe that building nice data software comes with an organized, clean and collaborative code. It is by sharing our innovations and good practices among the BNP Paribas data community that we can build smarter applications, together. So if you desire to be a contributor, feel free to send us an email or to come at the Datalab's co-working space on Unicity first floor.
