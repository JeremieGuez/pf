Few Words About PF DataLab

The Datalab was born in September 2016 as a result of the latest innovations in the tech industry. As a shared initiative, it is monitored through a steering committee made of 4 departments: Risks, Marketing, Operations and IT. The Datalab enables a smooth transition of the company data processes and services into the artificial intelligence area.

DataLab Platform

Sparrow is the first collaborative platform created by the Datalab, maintained and improved by the community. Sparrow is a platform fitted for Data Science needs.

Our Pilars


Successful Data Experiments.

As soon as you identify a potential interesting data project, feel free to come and meet the Datalab team to discuss about it. The Datalab offer guides you through all the different steps of your data projects by providing the necessary soft skills and hard skills. During the past months, the experiments done with DCM (B2B/B2C transformation), Risk (Credit Granting Score/Fraud Detection), and Ops (Broken promises/Best Time to Call) business lines have proven that we were capable of making successful data experiments.

A solid core engine.

To be able to work efficiently and to achieve great results, we believe it is necessary to have an appropriate infrastructure with state of the art data science tools. Following the latest devops practices, we developed internally the Sparrow platform together with the TPS team in Merignac, France and the ITG team in Montreuil, France. Sparrow currently runs two components: a Big Data platform that performs massive data storage and processing and a data science toolbox composed of the latest open-source machine learning libraries.

Data science expertise.

At the Datalab, we love to speak about mathematics, statistics, computer science, business, and communication. The Datalab team has acquired expertise in Data Science and is willing to help other internal teams to upskill as well. During experiments, you will be able to benefit from advice of our best internal and external Data Scientists. What is the most powerful machine learning algorithm? How to perform great feature engineering? We are here to answer your questions!

A dedicated co-working space.

We think that it is essential for teams to be united at a common workplace. We are now based on a dedicated co-working space in the PF Unicity building, in Paris - France. With up to 12 seats, this space will allow the team members to collaborate and get more done on a daily basis. If you do have a data project in mind but you still need help to move forward, to speed up your processes or to try new technologies, feel free to come around and tell us more about what you have in mind.
